Data Engineer — Nordic Freight Systems

Nordic Freight Systems is hiring a Data Engineer to build and maintain the
pipelines that power our logistics analytics platform.

Responsibilities:
- Design, build, and operate batch and streaming data pipelines
- Model warehouse tables for analysts and downstream ML teams
- Own data quality monitoring and alerting

Requirements:
- 3+ years working with Python and SQL in production
- Hands-on experience with Apache Airflow or Dagster
- Experience with a cloud data warehouse (Snowflake, BigQuery, or Redshift)
- Comfortable with Docker and CI/CD workflows

Nice to have:
- Kafka or other streaming experience
- dbt, Terraform
- Experience in logistics, supply chain, or transportation
