Embedded Software Engineer (C++) — Vektor Drive Systems

Vektor Drive Systems builds motor controllers for industrial robotics. We are
looking for an Embedded Software Engineer to work on the firmware that runs
our real-time control loops.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and maintain firmware in modern C++ (C++17) for ARM Cortex-M MCUs
- Implement and tune hard real-time control loops (current/velocity/position)
- Work with an RTOS (FreeRTOS or Zephyr) and bare-metal components
- Bring up new boards together with the hardware team (schematics, datasheets)
- Debug with oscilloscopes, logic analyzers, and JTAG/SWD tools

Requirements:
- 4+ years of professional C or C++ development for embedded targets
- Solid understanding of real-time constraints, interrupts, and DMA
- Experience with communication buses (CAN, SPI, I2C, UART)
- Comfortable reading schematics and using lab equipment
- Familiarity with unit testing and CI for embedded code

Nice to have:
- Motor control or power electronics background (FOC, PWM inverters)
- Functional safety exposure (IEC 61508, ISO 26262)
- Rust interest, MISRA C++ experience, or Yocto-based Linux work
